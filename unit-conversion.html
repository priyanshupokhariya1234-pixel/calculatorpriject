Unit Conversion

Introduction

Unit conversion is the process of converting a measurement from one unit to another. This is essential in science, engineering, and everyday life when working with different measurement systems.

The Unity Fraction Method

The best way to convert units is using "unity fractions" - fractions that equal 1, allowing you to multiply without changing the actual value.

Value in new unit = Value in old unit × (Conversion factor)

Key principle: Multiply by fractions that equal 1, canceling out unwanted units.

Length Conversions

Metric System

  • 1 kilometer (km) = 1000 meters (m)
  • 1 meter (m) = 100 centimeters (cm)
  • 1 centimeter (cm) = 10 millimeters (mm)

Imperial System

  • 1 mile = 5280 feet
  • 1 yard = 3 feet
  • 1 foot = 12 inches

Metric to Imperial

  • 1 meter ≈ 3.281 feet
  • 1 kilometer ≈ 0.621 miles
  • 1 inch = 2.54 centimeters

Mass/Weight Conversions

Metric System

  • 1 kilogram (kg) = 1000 grams (g)
  • 1 gram (g) = 1000 milligrams (mg)
  • 1 metric ton = 1000 kilograms

Imperial System

  • 1 ton = 2000 pounds
  • 1 pound (lb) = 16 ounces (oz)

Metric to Imperial

  • 1 kilogram ≈ 2.205 pounds
  • 1 pound ≈ 0.454 kilograms

Volume Conversions

Metric System

  • 1 liter (L) = 1000 milliliters (mL)
  • 1 cubic meter (m³) = 1000 liters

Imperial System

  • 1 gallon = 4 quarts
  • 1 quart = 2 pints
  • 1 pint = 2 cups

Metric to Imperial

  • 1 liter ≈ 0.264 gallons
  • 1 gallon ≈ 3.785 liters

Temperature Conversions

Celsius to Fahrenheit

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Fahrenheit to Celsius

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9

Celsius to Kelvin

K = °C + 273.15

Time Conversions

  • 1 minute = 60 seconds
  • 1 hour = 60 minutes = 3600 seconds
  • 1 day = 24 hours
  • 1 week = 7 days
  • 1 year = 365 days (or 366 in leap years)

Example: Converting Length

Problem: Convert 5 kilometers to miles

Solution:

  • 1 km ≈ 0.621 miles
  • 5 km × (0.621 miles / 1 km) = 3.105 miles
  • Note: The "km" units cancel out, leaving miles

Example: Converting Temperature

Problem: Convert 25°C to Fahrenheit

Solution:

  • °F = (25 × 9/5) + 32
  • °F = 45 + 32 = 77°F

Dimensional Analysis

Always write units with your numbers to keep track:

  • Good: 5 km × (1000 m / 1 km) = 5000 m
  • Bad: 5 × 1000 = 5000 (units unclear!)

Tips for Unit Conversion

  • Always write units next to numbers
  • Use unity fractions (fractions equal to 1)
  • Set up conversion so unwanted units cancel
  • Check your answer makes sense (e.g., meters should be larger than centimeters)
  • Memorize common conversions for your field
  • Double-check decimal point placement

Common Conversion Factors

  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm (exact)
  • 1 mile = 1.609 km
  • 1 pound = 0.454 kg
  • 1 gallon = 3.785 L
  • 1 acre = 43,560 square feet
