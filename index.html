Sine Function (sin θ)

The ratio of opposite side to hypotenuse in a right triangle. Periodic function with range [-1, 1].

Cosine Function (cos θ)

Ratio of adjacent side to hypotenuse. Phase-shifted version of sine.

Tangent Function (tan θ)

Ratio of sine to cosine. Has vertical asymptotes and period π.

Complex Numbers

Numbers in the form a + bi, where i² = -1. Essential in engineering.
